Our Story

A Place of Faith,
Family & Fellowship

Snow Hill Missionary Baptist Church has stood as a beacon of faith and fellowship in the heart of Roseboro, North Carolina for generations. Rooted in the rich traditions of the Baptist faith, our congregation is a family — bound together by love, service, and an unwavering commitment to God's Word.

We believe that the church is more than a building — it is the living body of Christ, called to serve our community with compassion, to worship with joy, and to grow together in grace. Whether you've been walking with Christ for decades or are taking your very first steps of faith, you will find a home here at Snow Hill.
